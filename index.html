My Bio

Nuake Justice Tsekpo Junior is a Microsoft Azure and Amazon Web Services cloud solutions architect. He also has a background in graphic design and writing. Aside from being a solutions architect, he is self-employed at NJR Graphics, a graphic design, freelance, and brand innovation company serving diverse businesses. Passionate about empowering small businesses through visual and digital design, Nuake also dedicates himself to improving the fitness and well-being of his community members.

Nuake's goal is to become a leading entrepreneur with a diversified portfolio of ventures spanning digital design, health and fitness, and education, potentially incorporating cloud technologies to enhance these areas. In the past year, he has increased his company's brand visibility by 200%. His innovative writing and design skills were recently showcased in the successful launch of a Black History Magazine. A Pan-Africanist at heart, Nuake enjoys traveling across the African continent.
